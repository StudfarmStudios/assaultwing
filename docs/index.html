Assault Wing is a free, action packed, ship-flying, gun blazing, explosive game of outwitting your opponents in arenas hidden deep in the far reaches of galaxies.

You will choose your ship, special weapon and ship modification and with these you will slaughter your friends and foes alike to pieces smaller than a nanometer.
